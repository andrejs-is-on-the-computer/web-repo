ABOUT US

At Narra Team we believe in the importance that strong collaborative relationships within a team have in dictating a business's success. We specialise in helping teams build connections between their members through professionally hosted storytelling games such as Dungeons and Dragons. Through our games we help individuals develop their interpersonal skills as well as improve communication, collaborative thinking and rapport between team members.
