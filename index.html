Narra Team

What is Narra Team?

At Narra Team we believe in the importance that strong collaborative relationships within a team have in dictating a business's success.

We specialise in helping teams build connections between their members through professionally hosted storytelling games such as Dungeons and Dragons.

Through our games we help individuals develop their interpersonal skills as well as improve communication, collaborative thinking and rapport between team members.

Who do we help?

Narra Team is ideal for small to medium teams of 2 to 8 people.

If you can segment your larger team into smaller collaborative groups we can also run multiple sessions simultaneously with each group.

When and where?

We come to you. No need to worry about coordinating the movement of your people to a new loaction, we just need a reasonably sized space that allows for some drinks and food to be consumed and we'll bring the rest.

We can host sessions during or after normal work hours.

Why choose Narra Team?

We have more then 3 years experience hosting storytelling games for a range of groups and individuals. With a passion for people and a focus on business we're here to help you get your team on track.

We have the ability to analyse personalities and intergroup dynamics and craft specific characters and situations to bring people closer together and build rapport through gaming.

Our approach makes team building fun, reduces team member stress and improves business productivity. It's a win for all involved!
